About us

Following the release of the horrifying video that documented the murder of George Floyd by the hands of the police, a revolution was born. Frustrations rose and the #BlackLivesMatter movement took centre stage worldwide for the first time since its inception. The allies gathered globally to show their intolerance for the racial injustice in the modern world. Following this, Black Lives Merchandise was established.

Our mission at Black Lives Merch is to encourage support of the #BlackLivesMatter movement through releasing items that can be used or worn day-to day or at a protest.

To ensure our customers' purchases make an impact on the movement, we donate a percentage of our proceeds to many charities fighting anti-racism,
including Stop Hate UK, Race Equality First and Kick it Out.
